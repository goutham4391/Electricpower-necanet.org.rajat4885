Student Chapter of the Year Award

  1. Home
  2. Membership
  3. Awards & Recognition
  4. Student Chapter of the Year Award

The National Electrical Contractors Association (NECA) launched the Student Chapter of the Year awards program in 2014 to recognize and reward outstanding NECA Student Chapters. The award was created to recognize and reward those students, faculty and NECA staff working hard year-round to run successful Student Chapters. These chapters are the ones promoting activities that expose students to the electrical contracting industry and also facilitate interaction between members and students.  

The award is presented annually at the NECA Convention and Trade Show.

For more information or questions, please contact Wanessa Alves.
