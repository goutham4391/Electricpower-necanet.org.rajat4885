Refund Policy

Cancellation policies for in-seat educational programs vary and are available at the time of registration. Generally, a minimum of 30 days advance notice is required to obtain a full or partial refund.

For online training, refunds or exchanges are available within 15 days of purchase if the course has not yet been accessed.
