Innovation and Strategy Task Force

The Innovation and Strategy Task Force seeks to elevate the construction industry through investigation and strategic application of industry trends and emerging technologies. The task force members, who are comprised of some of the top leaders in our industry, help navigate the future of construction while keeping our NECA contractors informed and prepared for the future of construction.

MISSION

To explore and educate NECA members on emerging industry innovations by providing best practices and resources for our contractors.

The Innovation and Strategy Task Force provides valuable information on the construction lifecycle and developing trends. Participating in mulit-trade coordination and keeping apprised on electrical innovations the task force explores future avenues for our contractors to succeed and become industry leaders. Through their work, they focus on initiatives led by their own shared needs as industry peers, supporting NECA members through value-added materials and programming, collaboration across the MEP trades, and innovation for all of the construction industry.
