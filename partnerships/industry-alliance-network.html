Industry Alliance Network

  1. Home
  2. Partnerships
  3. Industry Alliance Network

The electrical construction industry is evolving rapidly, and NECA is embracing new initiatives to ensure its members are prepared to face the challenges of today and tomorrow. With a renewed focus on education, innovation and technology, NECA is launching the Ambassador and Affiliate sponsorship programs. These programs—separate from NECA’s Premier Partnership agreements—will connect NECA members to a wide array of products and services designed to support their companies’ success and provide solutions with increased access and attention within our industry.

Limited spaces are available at each sponsorship level to ensure our member contractors are exposed to a diverse array of solution providers.

Four Pillars of the Industry Alliance Network

Networking & Promotion

Sponsors will get their brand in front of the NECA audience at events and programs throughout the year. This includes being featured at the annual NECA Convention and Trade Show, and invitations to key networking opportunities.

Name Recognition

Sponsors can leverage their NECA sponsorship to build clout and reputation within the electrical construction industry, creating direct relationships with NECA members.

Digital Marketing & Social Media

Sponsors gain an immediate, prominent presence in the publications and digital outlets that electrical contractors trust. This includes newsletters, print magazines, NECA’s web brands, impactful social media posts, and much more.

Communication & Education

Sponsors can get their message in front of electrical contractors through sponsored content, advertising, and webinars. This is an opportunity to deepen the conversation beyond logos and branding; companies can present their case directly to the audience.
