NECA Affiliate Sponsorship

  1. Home
  2. Partnerships
  3. NECA Affiliate Sponsorship

NECA's entry-level sponsorship program, the Affiliate Partner program gives organizations an opportunity to partner with NECA digitally with limited access to NECA's Contractor community.

Click here to download a brochure (PDF) for details on the benefits of becoming an Ambassador, or Affiliate. All sponsorships are two-year commitments, and companies are required to exhibit at the NECA Convention and Trade Show.

Limited spaces are available at each sponsorship level to ensure our member contractors are exposed to a diverse array of solution providers.
